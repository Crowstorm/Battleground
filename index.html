Battleground Scenery Simulator

Welcome to Battleground Scenery Simulator. After clicking the button program will roll dice for every square and determine how many terrain features will be used and will randomly place them. Then it will roll a single dice to give each piece of scenery an effect. You can display legend below the battleground to learn more about terrain and it's effects.

Dmn:
If any of your units are within 3'' of this terrain feature in your hero phase, you can declare that one is making a sacrifice. If you do so, the unit suffers D3 mortal wounds, but you can add 1 to all hit rolls for the unit until your next hero phase.
Arc:
Add 1 to the result of any casting or unbinding rolls made for a wizard within 3'' of this terrain feature
Ins:
Add 1 to the Bravery of all units within 3'' of this terrain feature.
Dead:
Roll a dice for any model that makes a run or charge move across, or finishing on this terrain feature. On a roll of 1 the model is slain.
Mst:
Roll a dice in your hero phase for each of your units within 3'' of this terrain feature. On a roll of 1 the unit is befuddled and can't be selected to cast spells, move or attack until your next hero phase. On a roll of 2-6 the unit is ensorcelled, and you can reroll failed wound rolls for the unit until your next hero phase.
Sin
Any of your units that are within 3'' of this terrain feature in your hero phase cause fear until your next hero phase. Substract 1 from the Bravery of any enemy units that are within 3'' of one of your units that cause fear.
